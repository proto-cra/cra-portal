CRA account help - Businesses

My Business Account overview – CRA account help - Businesses

You may be looking for:

  • About your profile – CRA account help
 

The overview page enables you to access your program account types (such as GST/HST, payroll or corporation income tax, etc.) associated with every Business number (BN) added to My Business Account. Outstanding returns and payments are highlighted for your quick reference. You can also view the status of any in-progress or recently completed submissions in the progress tracker.

On this page

  • Checking your business name and BN
  • If you have more than one business
  • Accessing your program accounts
  • If you have more than one program accounts of the same type

Checking your business name and BN

The legal name that appears on your BN registration records and the nine-digit BN are both displayed at the top right of the overview page.

Example: Business name and BN found at the top right 

ABC Industries Inc.
123456789

Your BN is also displayed at the top of the left-menu in the Business number (BN) drop-down.

If you have more than one business

If you have more than one BN added in My Business Account, you can select another BN from the drop-down options at the top of the left menu.

The information displayed on the overview page will correspond to the BN you select.

Example: You have two businesses that each have a payroll account

If you select the BN for your first business, the overview page will display the information for that business. Select the Payroll account (RP0001) for more payroll information related to that business.

If you select the BN for the second business, all the information displayed will apply to that business, including the payroll account (RP0001) information.

You can add or remove BNs, or choose which BN is displayed by default in your profile:

  1. In the left menu, select Profile
  2. Under the Business numbers heading, select Manage business number(s)

Learn more: About your profile – CRA account help

If you remove all the BNs

If you remove all the BNs from the Business numbers table, a BN will no longer appear on the overview page. Therefore, no services will be available. To regain these services, add a BN by selecting the Manage business number(s).

Accessing your program accounts

The left menu on the overview pages displays the program account types associated with your BN. Common program accounts include:

  • GST/HST (RT)
  • Payroll (RP)
  • Corporation income tax (RC)
  • Information Returns (RZ)
  • Registered charity (RR)
Other program accounts
  • Air travellers security charge (RG)
  • Compliance and Exchange (RZ)
  • Contract Payments (RZ)
  • COVID-19 rent subsidies (ZA)
  • Digital services tax (DT)
  • Excise duty (RD)
  • Excise tax (RE)
  • Excise tax on insurance premiums (RN)
  • Fuel charge (CT)
  • Luxury tax (LT)
  • Partnerships (RZ)
  • TFSA (RZ)
  • Underused housing tax (RU)

Learn more: Program accounts you may need

You can access each account from 2 locations on the overview page:

In the center column

Under the Accounts heading, a tile is displayed for each program account type associated with your BN. You can select the account link to view more information. For example, RT0001 for GST/HST or RP0001 for your Payroll.

In the left menu

All program account types associated with your BN are listed. Each one expands to list the different program accounts of the same type. For example, RT0001 for your GST/HST account.

Select the program account you want to access.

If you have more than one program accounts of the same type

When you select a program account type in the left menu, all your program accounts for this type are listed in a drop-down menu. You can then select the account you want to access.

Example: Your business has 2 GST/HST accounts 
  • GST/HST
    • RT0001
    • RT0002

Select one to display the information associated with that account.
